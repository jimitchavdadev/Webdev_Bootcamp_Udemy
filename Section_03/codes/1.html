Jimit's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 cup warm milk (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 eggs, room temperature
  • 1/3 cup margarine, melted
  • 4 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons bread machine yeast

For the filling:

  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup butter, softened

Instructions

  1. Warm the milk in a small saucepan until it bubbles, then remove from heat. Let cool until lukewarm.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine 2 1/4 cups flour and yeast.
  3. Add warm milk, eggs, margarine, salt, and sugar to the flour mixture. Mix well.
  4. Stir in the remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, beating well after each addition. When the dough has pulled together, turn it out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic, about 8 minutes.
  5. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let rest for 10 minutes. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon.
  6. Roll out the dough into a 12x9 inch rectangle. Spread dough with 1/3 cup butter and sprinkle evenly with sugar/cinnamon mixture. Roll up dough and cut into 12 rolls. Place rolls in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking pan. Cover and let rise until nearly doubled, about 30 minutes. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  7. Bake rolls in preheated oven until golden brown, about 20 minutes. Spread frosting on warm rolls before serving.
